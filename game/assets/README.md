# game/assets/ — where your game's ART lives

Right now this folder is **empty on purpose**.

In the ★ Bonus Mission — **Paint Your World** — you generate real 2D art with
AI (you write the prompt, pick the best of 4), and your chosen assets get saved
*here*, then wired into the game. Your very first asset lands in this folder.

Real games keep their images, sounds, and other assets in a folder just like
this one, separate from the code. Now yours has one too.
