# Module 6 — Speak AI Fluently

**Status:** OPENED by Dad (2026-07-07) · **Unlocks when Module 5 is complete.**
**Role:** Eli is Creative Director. Claude wears the **Prompt Coach** hat — it
coaches his prompting, then executes the prompts he sharpens.

**Version target:** the game climbs from **0.2 → 0.3** — real polish, driven by
prompts Eli wrote and sharpened himself.

## Purpose

Everything Eli does with AI runs through one skill: the prompt. This module makes
him genuinely good at it. He'll feel the gap between a lazy ask ("make it better")
and a sharp one (who, what, why, and the limits) — not as a lecture, but by
pointing both at his *real game* and watching the difference land.

This is a lifelong skill disguised as a game-polish session.

## The Brain-First Rule

**Eli writes the prompt first; the coach sharpens it after.** Don't hand him a
finished prompt. Have him attempt it, then improve it *together* — naming what
made the second version stronger so he can do it himself next time.

## What You'll Learn

- **Words are your controller** — the better the prompt, the better the game.
- "Make it better" gets you noise; **specifics** get you what you pictured.
- A great prompt names **WHO, WHAT, WHY, and the LIMITS.**
- Write your prompt first, *then* sharpen — don't outsource the thinking.
- Prompting is a real skill he'll use for the rest of his life.

## Rules for Claude Code in This Module

- Teach on **his real game** — every example prompt should aim at polishing
  *Thornwood Manor*, never an abstract exercise.
- **Show the contrast honestly:** when Eli gives a vague prompt, respond to what
  he *literally* said (a deliberately generic result), then let him feel why the
  sharper version is better. Don't secretly do a great job on a bad prompt.
- Build a tiny **"anatomy of a great prompt"** with him: specific target · his
  taste/intent · clear limits (what NOT to change).
- Let Eli **write and drive 2–3 real polish prompts** — his words. Land each one
  in the game and play the result.
- Keep changes safe and reversible; verify the game still solves after each.
- **Bump the version to 0.3** in `MEMORY.md`; add Session-Memory + `session-log`
  entries in Eli's voice.
- Update Module 6 Steps; unlock **AI Whisperer** at the end.

## Mission Flow

1. **Meet the Prompt Coach** — words as a controller.
2. **The weak prompt** — try "make my game better" and see the vague result.
3. **The strong prompt** — add who / what / why / limits; feel the difference.
4. **Anatomy of a great prompt** — specific, full of your taste, with clear limits.
5. **Sharpen a real one** — *Eli* writes a prompt to polish a real moment.
6. **Direct 2–3 polish prompts** into the game and see them land.
7. **Version bump** — sharper prompts, sharper game: **Version 0.3**.
8. **Achievement unlock:** AI Whisperer.

## Done When

- Eli has seen a weak prompt and a strong prompt aimed at the same goal.
- He's written and driven at least two real polish prompts in his own words.
- Those changes are live and the game still solves.
- The game is bumped to **Version 0.3** and the memory is updated.
- The **AI Whisperer** achievement is unlocked.
