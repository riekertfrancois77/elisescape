# Module 3 — Director Mode

**Status:** OPENED by Dad (2026-07-06) · **Unlocks when Module 2 is complete.**
**Role:** Eli is Creative Director. Claude is the studio's build team.

## Purpose

Eli has a playable game now (from Module 2). Time to stop being a passenger and
start *directing*. He plays his own game, decides what he doesn't like — a room,
a name, the mood, a rule — and makes the AI rebuild it **his** way. The lesson:
AI output is **clay, not stone**. His taste is the entire job.

## What You'll Learn

- AI's work is editable — nothing it makes is final.
- **Taste matters:** you decide what's good, boring, or wrong.
- You're allowed to **reject** AI's version and ask for another take.
- Small, **specific** change requests beat vague ones.
- Directing is *choosing between options*, not doing everything yourself.

## Rules for Claude Code in This Module

- Put Eli in charge. Offer **2–3 options** for each change and ask *"which feels
  most like your game?"* — don't just pick for him.
- Make **one change at a time**, and let him play/see it before the next.
- Never defend your own output — if he rejects it, cheerfully make another
  version. Rejecting AI is a skill worth praising.
- Keep every change true to the four pillars (Notice What's Off · Curiosity
  Rewarded / Cleverness Earned · A Duel With the Culprit · A Cozy House That
  Hides Everything). If a change fights a pillar, say so and let him decide.
- Record notable changes in `logs/decisions-made.md` and update `MEMORY.md`.

## Mission Flow

1. **Play & make a change list** — Eli plays his game and notes what feels off,
   boring, or not-him.
2. **Pick the top 3** changes worth making first.
3. **Direct one change** — he tells Claude what he wants; Claude offers versions.
4. **Choose the one** that feels most like *his* game.
5. **See it land**, play again, react.
6. **Repeat** for his top changes.
7. **Achievement unlock:** Director Mode.

## Done When

- Eli has directed at least a few real changes into his game.
- He's rejected at least one AI version and asked for a better one (that's the
  skill).
- The **Director Mode** achievement is unlocked and `MEMORY.md` is updated.
